# Ad Creation

> **Source:** Jon Loomer | **Last Updated:** 2026-04-28 | **Status:** Complete

---

## What It Is

The process of building individual ads with maximum creative diversity built in. The modern approach focuses on giving Meta many meaningful variations within each ad — different visuals, formats, text, and enhancements — so the delivery system can match the right version to the right person.

## Why It Matters

With Andromeda processing thousands of ad variations, giving Meta more options leads to better personalization and results. You don't need 50 separate ads — 2-3 well-built ads using all available variation tools can achieve full creative diversification.

## How It Works

### Text Variations
- Up to 5 manual primary text variations + 5 AI-generated = 10 total per ad
- Up to 5 manual headline variations + 5 AI-generated = 10 total
- Each text variation creates a potential version — Meta assembles the best combo per person

### Visual Diversity
- Use Flexible Format or Dynamic Creative to provide up to 10 images/videos in a single ad
- Accept AI-generated images and backgrounds — they can outperform originals (use the breakdown to verify)
- Accept Related Media — up to 10 additional creative variations from past ads
- Combine: up to 20 creative variations on top of your original upload

### Enhancements
- Enable Advantage+ Creative enhancements: overlays, visual touch-ups, translate text, animation, add music, enhance CTA, relevant comments
- These only activate when they'll improve performance — not applied universally
- Each enhancement generates a new potential version of your ad

### Placement Optimization
- Customize creative by placement — use 9x16 for Reels/Stories
- Use creative that can crop from 9x16 to 1x1 for feed placements

## Common Mistakes

- Creating many ads that look nearly identical (not truly diverse)
- Refusing AI-generated creative and enhancements without testing them
- Not using text variations (submitting a single primary text and headline)
- Ignoring Related Media suggestions
- Creating only one format (e.g., all static images, no video)

## How to Apply It

1. Start with 2-3 ads per ad set using Flexible Format
2. Upload 10 diverse images/videos per ad (mix formats: video, static, carousel)
3. Write 5 primary text variations targeting different pain points or personas
4. Write 5 headline variations
5. Accept or edit AI-generated text suggestions
6. Enable all Advantage+ Creative enhancements
7. Accept relevant Related Media suggestions
8. Customize creative by placement where possible

## When to Make Exceptions

- Brand-sensitive accounts may want to review AI-generated creative before accepting
- Very specific compliance requirements may limit which enhancements you can enable
- Single-product launches may start with fewer variations and expand
