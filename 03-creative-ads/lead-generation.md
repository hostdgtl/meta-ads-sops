# Lead Generation

> **Source:** Jon Loomer | **Last Updated:** 2026-04-28 | **Status:** Complete

---

## What It Is

Strategies for acquiring leads through Meta ads. This covers the choice between Sales and Leads objectives, Instant Forms, and quality optimization for lead campaigns.

## Why It Matters

Lead quality matters more than lead volume. A low cost-per-lead is meaningless if those leads never convert. Additionally, using the Sales objective when possible gives you access to Audience Segments — a critical reporting advantage.

## How It Works

1. **Sales vs. Leads objective:** Use Sales when possible (gives access to Audience Segments). Use Leads only when your conversion event is a form submission, not a purchase
2. **Instant Forms:** Meta's native lead forms that keep users on-platform. Fast submission but potentially lower quality
3. **Quality optimization:** Test "Higher Intent" Instant Forms that add a review step before submission
4. **Tailored Leads campaigns:** An alternative setup that Meta provides for simplified lead campaign construction
5. **For Shopify D2C:** Leads often come through email signups, quiz funnels, free shipping offers, or content downloads

## Common Mistakes

- Optimizing only for cost-per-lead without measuring downstream conversion rates
- Using the Leads objective when Sales would work (losing Audience Segments access)
- Not following up on leads quickly enough (speed to contact matters)
- Making Instant Forms too short — some friction improves quality

## How to Apply It

1. Determine if your goal is truly lead gen or if you can optimize for purchases (Sales objective)
2. If using Leads: set up Instant Forms with a review/confirmation step
3. Test Higher Intent forms against standard forms — compare lead quality, not just volume
4. Connect forms to your CRM immediately for fast follow-up
5. Track lead-to-customer conversion rate, not just cost-per-lead

## When to Make Exceptions

- B2B with long sales cycles where purchase tracking isn't feasible — Leads objective is appropriate
- Event registrations, webinar signups — Leads objective with Instant Forms works well
- High-consideration purchases where the first conversion is always a lead
