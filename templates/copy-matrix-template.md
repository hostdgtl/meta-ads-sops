## Copy Rules

- Write in the customer's language (from Phase 1 mining)
- Headlines: 5-8 words max — stop the scroll
- Hook text: 2-3 sentences — create tension or curiosity
- Body: Expand on the hook — deliver the value proposition
- CTA: Action-oriented, 2-5 words — tell them exactly what to do
- Minimum 20 unique variants per hook
- Each register should feel genuinely different, not just synonym-swapped

---

## Quality Check

- [ ] All copy uses customer verbatim language from the Master Brief
- [ ] Each register (witty/urgent/empathetic) has a distinct voice
- [ ] Headlines work standalone (would stop the scroll without body text)
- [ ] CTAs are specific (not generic "Learn More")
- [ ] No variant is a near-duplicate of another
- [ ] Copy works for all placements (Feed, Stories, Reels)
