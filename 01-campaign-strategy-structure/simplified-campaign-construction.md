# Simplified Campaign Construction

> **Source:** Jon Loomer | **Last Updated:** 2026-04-28 | **Status:** Complete

---

## What It Is

A modern approach to Meta campaign structure that reduces complexity at the campaign and ad set levels, shifting the strategic focus to ad creative. One campaign per goal, minimal ad sets, maximum creative diversity.

## Why It Matters

Overcomplicating structure wastes time, fragments budget, causes auction overlap, and fights the algorithm. A simplified structure lets Meta's delivery system optimize across a larger pool of creative and budget, while freeing you to focus on what actually drives results: the ads themselves.

## How It Works

1. Create one campaign per business goal (e.g., one Sales campaign, one Leads campaign)
2. Use Advantage+ Sales Campaign as the default campaign type
3. Keep ad sets to a minimum — you rarely need multiple ad sets for targeting purposes
4. Load each ad set with diverse creative (10-50 ads using Flexible Format, text variations, etc.)
5. Let Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) distribute spend automatically
6. Monitor results via Audience Segments breakdown, not by splitting audiences into separate ad sets

## Common Mistakes

- Creating separate ad sets for remarketing vs. prospecting (remarketing happens naturally)
- Splitting audiences by interest, age, or gender into different ad sets
- Running too many campaigns competing against each other
- Spending all strategic time on structure instead of creative

## How to Apply It

- Audit your current account: consolidate campaigns with the same goal
- Move to Advantage+ Sales Campaign for purchase-focused goals
- Collapse multiple ad sets into one with diverse creative
- Use Audience Segments to verify delivery distribution instead of controlling it through structure

## When to Make Exceptions

- Use separate campaigns for genuinely different objectives (Sales vs. Leads vs. Awareness)
- Consider ABO with separate ad sets for structured creative testing
- Geographic splits may warrant separate ad sets if budgets differ by region
