# Value Rules

> **Source:** Jon Loomer | **Last Updated:** 2026-05-18 | **Status:** Complete

---

## What It Is

Value Rules let you tell Meta which conversions are worth more to your business by applying bid multipliers to specific audience groups. The newer Value Rules for Audiences feature lets you bid more or less based on custom audience groups, age ranges, or gender — without restricting your audience.

## Why It Matters

You have information Meta doesn't have about which customers are most valuable. Value Rules bridge this gap — they steer delivery toward higher-value segments while keeping broad Advantage+ targeting intact. This is the preferred alternative to hard audience restrictions.

## How It Works

1. In campaign settings, set rules that tell Meta to bid higher for certain groups
2. Audience-based rules let you bid more for custom audiences or demographic groups (age, gender)
3. Unlike hard restrictions, Value Rules don't exclude anyone — they adjust bid aggressiveness
4. Works with conversion optimization to steer toward higher-value outcomes
5. **Example:** Wine brand sees 40% of A+ spend going to 65+. Set a bid multiplier valuing 25-40 conversions higher. Meta shifts delivery without shrinking the audience pool
6. **Updated UI (May 2026):** Value Rules are now organized into two categories — **Conversion Location** (Website, App, Instant Forms) and **Placements**. Rules can also be added directly from the Placements section of the ad set, not just from campaign settings

## Common Mistakes

- Using hard age/gender restrictions when Value Rules would achieve the same goal
- Not knowing Value Rules exist and accepting skewed delivery
- Confusing Value Rules with Audience Segments (segments = reporting; rules = delivery)

## How to Apply It

- Look for Value Rules or Value Rules for Audiences in campaign settings, or add them directly from the Placements section of the ad set
- Identify which customer segments drive the most revenue or LTV
- Set bid multipliers for those segments
- Monitor delivery via Audience Segments and demographic breakdowns
- Adjust multipliers based on results over 1-2 weeks

## When to Make Exceptions

- If your product appeals to all demographics equally, Value Rules may not be needed
- If legal compliance requires hard restrictions (special ad categories), use Audience Controls instead
