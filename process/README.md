# AI-Driven Creative Process for Meta Ads

**Standard Operating Procedure | Host Digital | Internal Use Only**

---

## Core Principle

> Creative is the new targeting.

The Meta algorithm is now sophisticated enough that broad targeting works — but only if you feed it enough creative diversity to find its own signal. The team's job is no longer to pick the winner. It's to produce enough well-structured creative variants that the algorithm can pick for us.

Treat creative production as a **supply chain problem** — not a singular artistic endeavour.

---

## The Ad Family Model

Rather than producing one-off ads, we produce **Ad Families**: groups of ads that explore the same core insight through different formats and visual styles.

Each family contains:

- A **core hook or angle** (Problem/Solution, FOMO, Social Proof, etc.)
- **Multiple format variants** (UGC-style, Studio, Founder-to-camera, Static)
- **Multiple copy variants** (witty, urgent, empathetic)
- **Multiple visual treatments** (warm, minimal, bold)

---

## The 3-Phase Workflow

| Phase | Name | Key Output | Time Estimate |
|-------|------|------------|---------------|
| 1 | [Research & The Magic Brief](phase-1-research-magic-brief.md) | Master Brief with 5-10 hooks | 2-4 hours |
| 2 | [Modular Production](phase-2-modular-production.md) | 20+ copy variants, 10+ visuals, 2-3 videos | 1-3 days |
| 3 | [The Feedback Loop](phase-3-feedback-loop.md) | Creative Learnings Log + Expand/Explore decision | Ongoing (weekly) |

---

## Guiding Principles

1. **Conceptual Diversity over Iterative Tweaks** — different angles, not just different colours
2. **Expand vs. Explore** — scale winners or return to Phase 1 for new angles
3. **Creative Scoring** — use data to understand what element is driving performance, not just which ad won
4. **Supply Chain Thinking** — a steady pipeline of new creative beats a single "hero" ad
