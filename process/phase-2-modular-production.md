# Phase 2: Modular Production

Generate a high-volume, diverse set of creative assets from the Master Brief.

---

## Objective

Produce a full set of creative components — visuals, copy, and video — that can be assembled into **Ad Families**. Think modular: each element is interchangeable.

---

## Step 1 — Visual Production

Generate high-fidelity product photography in varied environments from a single reference image using AI image generation (Nano Banana 2, Midjourney, or equivalent).

### Visual Variants to Produce per Hook

- 3-5 **background/environment variants** (bathroom shelf, gift table, outdoor flat lay)
- 2-3 **lighting treatments** (warm, cool, editorial)
- 1-2 **lifestyle context shots** (in-use, hands holding, unwrapping)
- 1 **clean product-on-white** for retargeting

### Prompt Structure for AI Image Generation

```
Product: [product name].
Style: [editorial/lifestyle/UGC].
Environment: [bathroom/kitchen/outdoor].
Lighting: [warm golden hour/cool daylight/soft studio].
Mood: [calm/indulgent/joyful].
Do NOT include text or branding in the image.
```

### Quality Checklist

- [ ] Product is clearly recognisable and accurately represented
- [ ] No AI artifacts (extra fingers, warped text, impossible geometry)
- [ ] Lighting is consistent within each Ad Family
- [ ] Images work at mobile dimensions (1080x1080, 1080x1350, 1080x1920)

---

## Step 2 — Copy Matrix

For each hook in the Master Brief, produce a **Copy Matrix** of 20+ variants across three emotional registers. This is the core creative supply chain.

### Matrix Structure

| Copy Element | Witty | Urgent | Empathetic |
|-------------|-------|--------|------------|
| Primary Headline | Your skin called. It wants a word. | Only 48 hours left. Don't miss out. | Your skin deserves better than synthetic. |
| Primary Text (Hook) | We spent years perfecting 4 ingredients so you don't have to read a 40-word label. | The Christmas collection is almost gone. 3 days. Then it's gone until next year. | If your skin has been reacting to products, it might not be your skin. It might be the ingredients. |
| CTA | Meet the bar. | Shop now — before it's gone. | Try it. Feel the difference. |

### Copy Rules

- Each hook gets its own matrix (5-10 hooks = 5-10 matrices)
- Minimum 20 unique copy variants per hook
- Headlines: 5-8 words max
- Primary text: 2-3 sentences for the hook, expand in body if needed
- CTA: Action-oriented, 2-5 words
- Always write in the customer's language (from Phase 1 mining)

Use the [Copy Matrix Template](../templates/copy-matrix-template.md) to produce each matrix.

## Step 3 — Video Production

Video is optional but high-impact. Prioritise short, scroll-stopping formats. Use AI video tools (Veo 2, Sora 2) for product-in-motion clips or ambient scene-setting.

### Recommended Video Formats

| Format | Duration | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Scroll Stopper | 6 seconds | Single bold claim over product close-up. No voiceover needed. |
| Problem/Solution | 15 seconds | 3s on problem, 10s on product experience, 2s on CTA. |
| UGC-style | 30 seconds | Conversational delivery, as if speaking to a friend. AI-generated via InVideo AI or ElevenLabs voiceover over visuals. |

### Video Checklist

- [ ] Hook appears in first 2 seconds (text overlay or visual)
- [ ] Product is visible by second 3
- [ ] CTA is clear in final 2 seconds
- [ ] Works with sound off (captions/text overlays)
- [ ] Exported in 9:16 (Stories/Reels), 1:1 (Feed), 16:9 (optional)

---

## Deliverable

A full **Creative Inventory** per campaign:

- 20+ copy variants organised by hook and emotional register
- 10+ visual assets across environments and treatments
- 2-3 video clips in recommended formats
- All assets tagged by Ad Family, hook, format, and register

1-3 days per product/campaign.
