# Detailed Targeting and Lookalike Audiences

> **Source:** Jon Loomer | **Last Updated:** 2026-04-28 | **Status:** Complete

---

## What It Is

Detailed targeting (interests, behaviors, demographics) and lookalike audiences are legacy targeting tools that still exist but now function primarily as suggestions to Meta's algorithm rather than hard boundaries.

## Why It Matters

When optimizing for conversions, both detailed targeting and lookalike audiences are automatically expanded via Advantage+ Detailed Targeting and Advantage+ Lookalike. Meta will go beyond these inputs to find people likely to convert. Understanding this prevents wasted effort on audience curation.

## How It Works

1. Detailed targeting lets you specify interests, behaviors, and demographics
2. Lookalike audiences model new users based on a source audience (e.g., purchasers)
3. With Advantage+ enabled (default for conversions), both are treated as starting signals — Meta expands beyond them
4. Advantage+ Audience takes this further: your inputs are "suggestions" with no guaranteed boundaries
5. Testing consistently shows broad targeting (no inputs) performs equal to or better than detailed targeting

## Common Mistakes

- Spending hours curating interest-based audiences that Meta will expand anyway
- Believing narrow audiences give you more control (they don't with Advantage+ on)
- Stacking too many interests that overlap and fragment delivery
- Not testing broad vs. detailed to see if inputs actually matter for your account

## How to Apply It

- Test Advantage+ Audience (no inputs) against your best interest/lookalike audiences
- Run both for 2-4 weeks with equal budgets and compare cost per conversion
- If results are similar, drop the inputs — they're adding complexity without value
- If you use inputs, keep them broad (category-level interests, not hyper-specific)

## When to Make Exceptions

- Brand-new accounts with zero conversion data may benefit from lookalike seeds
- Very niche products where algorithmic targeting has no signal to work with
- Testing phases where you want to understand which audiences respond differently
